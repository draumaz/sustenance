Sustenance is a private, open-source window into the health and fitness data stored
in Android's Health Connect. It only ever reads, nothing you see leaves your device,
and there are no accounts, ads, or trackers.

Features:

* Today dashboard, Material You cards for nutrition, energy, and more, each with an
  at-a-glance sparkline.
* Detail views, tap any metric for a full chart, statistics and recent records.
* Weekly summary, daily averages measured against goals you set, with progress rings
  and week-over-week trends.
* Home-screen widgets, your key numbers, themed to match your wallpaper.
* Export, save your accessible data to a CSV or JSON file at any time.

Sustenance reads data through Health Connect, so it works alongside whatever apps and
wearables already write there. You stay in control of exactly which data types it can
read, and can change or revoke access at any time from Health Connect.
